Why MatchUp?

The objective of this app is to bring together ESSEC students who share common interests and values, encouraging them to participate in collective activities and promoting a sense of community among students aged 17 to 24. With the goal of overcoming potential exclusion resulting from the diverse backgrounds of ESSEC students, MatchUp aims to improve the overall student experience and foster a more inclusive environment.
